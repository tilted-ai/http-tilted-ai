Research

Current work focuses on Perceiver-like transformers endowed with various novel forms of compressed memory. In addition, generalizations to Perceivers with higher-dimensional attention are considered based on Simplicial and AlphaFold-inspired mechanisms. We welcome anyone with the capacity to contribute.
